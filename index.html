Assessment Framework
Four-Dimensional Scoring Model

Overcoming single-metric blind spots by integrating four complementary dimensions for a holistic view of resident-family dynamics and regulatory visitation compliance.

Dimension 01

Social Network Breadth

Diversity of unique visitors per resident — family, friends, and community connections over rolling time windows.

📊 Unique visitor count / month
Dimension 02

Interaction Intensity

Total visitation frequency capturing the depth of family engagement regardless of visitor diversity.

📊 Total visits / month
Dimension 03

Relationship Stability

Month-over-month consistency via sliding-window trend analysis to detect gradual engagement decay.

📊 3-month moving average variance
Dimension 04

Immediate Attention

AI-driven alert layer flagging sudden visitation drops requiring urgent social worker intervention.

📊 AI-triggered decline alerts

Replicability & Scalability

The entire platform runs on a serverless cloud architecture with zero hardware cost. Customizable scoring thresholds and modular design enable any LTC institution to deploy with minimal technical expertise — addressing digital transformation barriers for small-to-medium care facilities globally.

Family Visitation
Cloud-Based Visitor Registration & Engagement

The family-facing mobile portal serves as the primary data capture layer — transforming routine check-ins into structured multi-dimensional assessment data.

Self-Service Family Portal

Families access the system via a personalized URL (distributed through messaging apps). No account creation required — identity embedded in the link for zero-friction adoption.

  • 📅
    Visit Scheduling — Calendar-based slot booking with capacity management and real-time confirmation
  • 📋
    TOCC Health Screening — Travel, Occupation, Contact, Cluster declaration for infection control compliance
  • ⭐
    Visit Point Card — Gamified monthly visit tracker encouraging consistent engagement — directly feeds Dimension 2
  • 📊
    Compliance Rate — Booking vs. actual registration ratio — data integrity metric for the assessment model

Data → Dimensions Mapping

Every registration event automatically feeds the 4D model: unique visitors → D1 Breadth, total visits → D2 Intensity, monthly patterns → D3 Stability, sudden drops → D4 Attention.

Social Worker Screening
AI-Driven Risk Detection & Heatmap Visualization

The core innovation — transforming raw visitation data into actionable risk intelligence. In qualitative interviews, 90% of staff reported heatmaps "significantly improved screening efficiency."

Heatmap-Based Visual Screening

The heatmap provides an instant, at-a-glance view of every resident's family engagement across months. Color intensity maps directly to the AI's 4-tier risk classification:

  • 🟢
    Tier 1 — Adequate: Consistent engagement, no action needed
  • 🟡
    Tier 2 — Monitor: Mild decline detected, passive observation
  • 🟠
    Tier 3 — Alert: Significant drop, social worker outreach initiated
  • 🔴
    Tier 4 — Critical: Zero visits detected, immediate intervention required
📞

Conclusion
Proactive Care Through Digital Innovation

This study demonstrates that meaningful digital transformation in LTC doesn't require massive budgets. By embedding AI-driven multi-dimensional assessment into existing workflows, we shift from reactive responses to proactive risk management — ensuring no vulnerable resident is left without timely intervention.

Global Applicability
