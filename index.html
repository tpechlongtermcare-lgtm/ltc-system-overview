Assessment Framework
Four-Dimensional Scoring Model

Overcoming single-metric blind spots by integrating four complementary dimensions for a holistic view of resident-family dynamics and regulatory visitation compliance.

Dimension 01

Social Network Breadth

Diversity of unique visitors per resident — family, friends, and community connections over rolling time windows.

📊 Unique visitor count / month
Dimension 02

Interaction Intensity

Total visitation frequency capturing the depth of family engagement regardless of visitor diversity.

📊 Total visits / month
Dimension 03

Relationship Stability

Month-over-month consistency via sliding-window trend analysis to detect gradual engagement decay.

📊 3-month moving average variance
Dimension 04

Immediate Attention

AI-driven alert layer flagging sudden visitation drops requiring urgent social worker intervention.

📊 AI-triggered decline alerts
